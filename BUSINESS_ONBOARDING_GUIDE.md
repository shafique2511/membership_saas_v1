# Business Onboarding Guide

## Goal

Use this checklist when preparing a real barber shop, coffee shop, or similar appointment and POS business for launch.

## Setup Checklist

1. Create the business and owner account.
2. Select the correct package.
3. Configure business profile, logo, timezone, currency, and contact details.
4. Add branches.
5. Add services, resources, staff, and schedules.
6. Add membership plans and loyalty rewards.
7. Add products, cost prices, selling prices, and stock quantities.
8. Configure payment methods.
9. Configure WhatsApp and notification templates.
10. Set staff permissions.
11. Review package usage limits.
12. Export a test backup from Settings > Data & Backup.

## Launch Validation

Run these checks before the business starts using the system:

- Owner can log in.
- Staff can only access permitted modules.
- Customer portal opens for the correct business.
- Booking slot conflicts are blocked.
- Membership balance deducts correctly.
- POS checkout reduces inventory.
- Payment status updates reports.
- Loyalty points are earned after payment.
- Reviews can be requested after completed bookings or orders.
- Mobile owner view works for booking, check-in, POS, membership scan, sales summary, and notifications.
- Business export only includes the current business.

## Training Notes

Train owners and managers on:

- Daily booking flow
- Customer check-in
- POS checkout
- Membership usage
- Refund handling
- Review moderation
- Upgrade page and package limits
- Data export and backup responsibility
- Audit log review
